Lentil Soup

Vegan and Gluten Free!

This simple recipe is packed full of flavour with plenty of protein and fibre. Perfect for warming you up on a cold winter's day or evening.

Makes 8 portions that can be refridgerated for up to 1 week. Also suitable for home freezing.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sunflower oil
  • 200g chopped oinion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 350g carrots, scrubbed and diced
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • 350g red lentils
  • 4 red peppers,deseededand chopped
  • 2.30 litres gluten free vegetables stock
  • freshlyground black pepper
  • optional chopped parsley to garnish

Preperation

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan, then add the prepared onion, celery and carrots.
    Cook for approx 2 minutes then add the tumeric and stir in for 1 further minute.
  2. Add the red lentils, red peppers and stock. Stir and bring to the boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add black pepper, then blend soup using a hand blender until it's a smooth puree.
  5. Serve adding garnish if desired.
    Alternatively allow to cool and store in individual portion sized containers in fridge or freezer until desired. These can simply be reheated using a microwave.
